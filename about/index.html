LUCA BERMEJO // NONE

Hello there, I'm a Multimedia Artist, Entrepreneur, Web Developer and Advocate based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, looking for opportunities to relocate to Europe.

One of my latest projects is as COO & Partner of an app in development called 'QUIR', aimed at ensuring safe spaces for the LGBTQIA+ community. Right now we’re working on the finishing touches of our MVP!

I am currently studying a Diploma in Web Development, alongside with a Bachelor in Electronic Arts, and working as an IT Technician.

I appreciate Clean and Polished Designs, with a special taste for Minimalism, Bauhaus and Neo-Brutalism.

Lastly, I speak English and Spanish fluently, a bit of French, and I’m actually learning German and Norwegian.

Thanks for reading up to here!

Warm Regards, Luca
